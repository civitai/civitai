### **Overview**
We are seeking a detail-oriented Administrative Coordinator to manage our back-office operations. This role is ideal for someone with bookkeeping experience who thrives in a fast-paced startup environment and can work independently with minimal supervision.

You'll be responsible for accounts receivable and payable, payroll coordination, vendor management, and general administrative support. This is a foundational role that keeps our operations running smoothly while our team focuses on building the product.

### **Key Responsibilities**
* Process accounts receivable and accounts payable accurately and on schedule.
* Coordinate payroll processing and ensure timely payments to team members and contractors.
* Manage vendor relationships, including correspondence, invoice tracking, and payment scheduling.
* Maintain organized financial records and documentation.
* Handle general administrative tasks as needed (scheduling, correspondence, document management).
* Reconcile accounts and flag discrepancies for review.
* Assist with basic financial reporting and expense tracking.
* Coordinate with external accountants during tax season and audits as needed.

### **Requirements**
* 2+ years of experience in bookkeeping, accounting, or financial administration.
* Proficiency with accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, or similar).
* Experience with payroll platforms (Gusto, Rippling, Deel, or similar).
* Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
* Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities.
* Excellent written communication skills.
* Reliable internet connection and home office setup for remote work.
* Based in a timezone compatible with US Pacific hours (some overlap required).

### **Nice to Have**
* Experience working with startups or small tech companies.
* Familiarity with contractor management and international payments.
* Experience with project management tools (ClickUp, Notion, or similar).
* Basic understanding of SaaS business models and subscription billing.
* Bookkeeping certification or accounting degree.

### **About the Role**
This is a part-time contractor position paid hourly. We're looking for someone who can commit to consistent weekly hours and become a reliable part of our operations. The ideal candidate is proactive, communicates clearly, and doesn't need hand-holding to get work done.
