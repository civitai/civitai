---
title: 🚫 Access Restricted for Australian Visitors
description: Civitai is no longer accessible to users in Australia due to the Online Safety Act.
---

As of March 16, 2026, Civitai is no longer accessible to users in Australia.

This is due to Australia's Online Safety Act, which imposes strict legal requirements on all platforms with user-generated content. These include age verification mandates, complex compliance obligations, and significant penalties for non-compliance. These rules apply even to platforms based outside Australia.

This is not a decision we made lightly. We began looking into what compliance would involve, but quickly realized it is not something we can feasibly manage with a team of our size. The legal and financial burden is simply too great.

We are heartbroken to block access, and we know this is upsetting. If you are an Australian citizen, we encourage you to contact your local Member of Parliament and share your concerns about how the Online Safety Act affects access to art, technology, and online communities. You can also learn more at the [eSafety Commissioner's website](https://www.esafety.gov.au/).

We are truly sorry, and we hope to return in the future.
Thank you for being part of the Civitai community.
