Standards for cosmetics submitted to the Cosmetic Shop. There are three types: Badges, Profile Backgrounds, and Avatar Decorations.

## Content & Safety (all cosmetic types)

- Cosmetics must be **safe-for-work**, matching the “SFW only” notice at the top of the submission modal. They show across the whole site (profiles, badges, avatar frames), so they need to stay SFW everywhere, including on civitai.red.
- Keep imagery at a **PG / PG-13** level. No mature, sexual, gory, or otherwise NSFW content.
- No **copyrighted or trademarked** material you don’t own.

## Badges

- Built on a hexagon frame. Elements can break out of the hexagon, but the hexagon stays the recognizable base.
- Keep the hexagon a consistent size so badges sit together cleanly.
- If a design cannot work as a hexagon, it probably is not a badge.

## Profile Backgrounds

- Keep them light on text. A single piece of large text is fine and stays readable behind the profile stats.
- Avoid backgrounds filled with small text meant to be read. That is too much.
- Aim for effects and imagery that sit behind the creator card without competing with the content.

## Avatar Decorations

- At least 50% of the avatar stays visible. Decorations frame the image, they do not cover the center.
- Intentional effects like a censor bar are fine as long as most of the avatar shows and the effect is clearly the point.
